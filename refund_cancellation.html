Refund & Cancellation Policy

This Refund and Cancellation Policy explains how you can cancel an order or request a refund for any product or service purchased through our Platform.

Order Cancellation

  • Cancellations will be accepted only if the request is made within 2 days of placing the order.
  • Cancellation requests may not be accepted if the seller/merchant has already processed or shipped the order, or if the product is out for delivery.
  • If cancellation is not possible, you may reject the product at the time of delivery.

Non-Cancellable Products

Orders for perishable items such as flowers, eatables, etc. cannot be cancelled. However, a refund or replacement may be processed if it is proven that the delivered product was of poor quality.

Damaged or Defective Products

  • If you receive a damaged or defective item, please report it to our customer service team within 2 days of receiving the product.
  • The seller/merchant will verify the issue before taking any action.
  • If the product does not match the listing or your expectations, notify us within 2 days, and the customer service team will take an appropriate decision.

Products with Manufacturer Warranty

For products covered by a manufacturer warranty, please contact the manufacturer directly for service, repair, or replacement.

Refund Timeline

For approved refunds, BIBEKANANDA PAL will process the refund within 7 days. Refunds will be made to the original payment method unless otherwise specified.
